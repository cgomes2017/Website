Red Flags

Red Flags is a clothing brand that my best friend and I have started. Both of us have always had a major interest in fashion and have decided to work together on this project. I also felt that starting a small business like this would be good practice for me since I am interested in creating my own mobile application. I have so far learned a few skills in digital and social media marketing as well as product development. Working on this project has also showed me the importance of building a strong relationship with customers. I feel that these skills will be very important when it is time for me to launch my mobile application.
