HIGHMAJOR

Highmajor is an application that I have been creating. It is focused on helping underrecruited high school athletes get noticed by college coaches across all three levels. Many recruiting services charge consumers hundreds and sometimes even thousands of dollars to use. Many athletes come from families that cannot afford these prices which sometimes causes them to go unnoticed Highmajor will be a free-to-use service for these athletes. The app will be similar to Instagram where users can easily connect with each other which will help athletes get noticed. This application is currently in development and I am hoping to have it completed by Summer 2021.
